## Ground rules (apply to everything)

1. **Turn on two-factor authentication (2FA)** on every account below. Use an authenticator app (Google Authenticator, Authy, or 1Password) — not SMS. SMS codes can be intercepted via SIM-swap.
2. **Use a password manager.** 1Password, Bitwarden, or Dashlane. Every password should be unique and at least 20 characters.
3. **Use your business email** `darren@darrenjpaul.com` for every platform sign-up — never a personal Gmail. If you ever leave a service, the account goes with the business, not you.
4. **Never share passwords with me.** Instead, add me as a limited-role admin on each platform. That way you can revoke my access instantly without having to change any password.
5. **Back up your 2FA recovery codes.** Every platform gives you 8–10 one-time codes when you enable 2FA — print them and store them offline (safe or locked drawer). If you lose your phone without these, you're locked out for weeks.
6. **Add a backup admin** wherever the platform supports it (trusted family member or co-owner). This is the single most important thing you can do — it's your safety net if you're ever locked out or compromised.
7. **Review connected apps monthly.** Every platform has a "Connected Apps" or "Business Integrations" screen. Anything you don't recognize, remove.
8. **You remain the primary admin on everything.** I'll be added as a limited admin — never the owner.
