Use this reference when filling in columns I through P (the AI metadata columns).
The first 8 columns (A-H) are filled by the coach. Your job is to fill columns I-P.

COLUMN REFERENCE
================

I. movement_pattern (pick ONE)
   Values: push | pull | squat | hinge | lunge | carry | rotation | isometric | locomotion
   Leave blank if none clearly applies (e.g. some recovery exercises)

J. primary_muscles (pipe-separated, pick 1-3)
   Values: chest | upper_back | lats | shoulders | biceps | triceps | forearms | core | obliques | lower_back | glutes | quadriceps | hamstrings | calves | hip_flexors | adductors | abductors | traps | neck
   Example: quadriceps|glutes

K. secondary_muscles (pipe-separated, pick 0-3)
   Same values as primary_muscles.
   Example: hamstrings|core
   Leave blank if no secondary muscles

L. force_type (pick ONE)
   Values: push | pull | static | dynamic
   - push: pressing movements (bench press, squat drive phase)
   - pull: pulling movements (rows, deadlifts, curls)
   - static: isometric holds, anti-movement exercises
   - dynamic: explosive or rhythmic movements (swings, jumps, runs)

M. laterality (pick ONE)
   Values: bilateral | unilateral | alternating
   - bilateral: both sides work together (barbell squat, bench press)
   - unilateral: one side at a time (single-leg RDL, pistol squat)
   - alternating: sides alternate (walking lunges, lateral band walk)

N. equipment_required (pipe-separated)
   Values: barbell | dumbbell | kettlebell | cable_machine | smith_machine | resistance_band | pull_up_bar | bench | squat_rack | leg_press | leg_curl_machine | lat_pulldown_machine | rowing_machine | treadmill | bike | box | plyo_box | medicine_ball | stability_ball | foam_roller | trx | landmine | sled | battle_ropes | agility_ladder | cones | yoga_mat
   Example: barbell|squat_rack
   Leave blank for bodyweight exercises

O. is_bodyweight
   Values: true | false
   true = no external load needed (push-ups, pull-ups, planks)

P. is_compound
   Values: true | false
   true = multi-joint exercise (squat, deadlift, bench press)
   false = single-joint isolation (bicep curl, leg extension, lateral raise)
